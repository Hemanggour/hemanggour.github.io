About Me

Hi, I'm Hemang Gour, an AI engineer and backend developer from India, passionate about building intelligent systems that reason, adapt, and scale.

My core interest lies in large language models, context-aware AI agents, and tool-augmented reasoning, where I work with frameworks like LangChain, LangGraph and Vector databases to create systems that go beyond just responding - they think.
